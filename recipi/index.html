Gulab Jamun Delight

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk powder
  • Flour
  • Cardamom pods

Recipe

  1. Combine milk powder, flour, and a pinch of baking soda in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Shape the dough into small balls and deep-fry them until golden brown.
  4. In a separate pan, heat sugar, water, and crushed cardamom pods to make a syrup.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the syrup for 30 minutes before serving.

Rasgulla Bliss

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Rose water

Recipe

  1. Boil milk and add lemon juice to curdle it.
  2. Strain the curdled milk and squeeze out excess whey.
  3. Knead the cheese until smooth and divide into small balls.
  4. In a separate pan, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water.
  5. Add rose water and gently place the cheese balls in the syrup.
  6. Cover and cook for 15 minutes on low heat until the balls expand.
  7. Allow them to cool before serving.

Kaju Katli Delight

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Saffron strands

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews to a fine powder.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee and add the cashew powder.
  3. Add sugar and cook on low heat, stirring continuously.
  4. When the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan, remove from heat.
  5. Mix in saffron strands for flavor and color.
  6. Spread the mixture on a greased plate and let it cool.
  7. Cut into diamond-shaped pieces and serve.

Jalebi Twist

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yogurt
  • Saffron strands
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Prepare a batter by mixing all-purpose flour, yogurt, and water.
  2. Add saffron strands and cardamom powder to enhance flavor.
  3. Let the batter ferment for a few hours or overnight.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan and pour the batter into a piping bag.
  5. Squeeze the batter in circular motions to form spiral-shaped jalebis.
  6. Fry until golden brown and soak in sugar syrup for a few minutes.
  7. Drain excess syrup and serve hot.
